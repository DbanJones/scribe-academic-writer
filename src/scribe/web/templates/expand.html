What Expand will and won't do

Preserved exactly

  • Every existing claim and its stance
  • Every citation, number, percentage, date, unit
  • Every figure and table reference
  • Your terminology, framework, and coined constructs
  • Section structure and heading hierarchy

Added depth

  • Unpack compressed claims into their mechanisms
  • Surface evidence implied but not stated
  • Address counter-arguments that belong in scope
  • Define assumed terms; spell out methodology
  • Add context where claims start cold

Never: invent sources, add claims the author didn't make, drift from the original thesis, or contradict any existing statement.
